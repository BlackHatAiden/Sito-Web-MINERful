Compliance checking

Starting from the construction of a model in this case a Workflow net verifies the consistency of it with the constraints defined by the modeling of the reality in question by the specifications. In this example is used a Workflow net that is a particular Petri net (P,T,F) where :

  1. there exists one and only one input place pi ∈ P such that pi = ∅

  2. there exists one and only one output place po ∈ O such that po = ∅

  3. every node x ∈ P ∪ T is on a walk from pi to po

  4. the initial marking µ0 is such that µ0(pi) = 1 and µ0(pi) = 0 for every p ∈ P\{pi}

  5. the final marking set is a singleton MF = {µF} where µF(po) = 1 and µF(p') = 0 for every p'∈ P\{po}

Declarative specification of a process model


  1. Check stock availability must occur right after the start.

    1. Right after Check stock availability, either Manufacture product.


    2. Right after Check stock availability, Retrieve product from the warehouse will occur.


  2. Confirm order may occur once only after Manufacture product or Retrieve product from the warehouse.


    1. Emit invoice must occur once after Confirm order.

      1. Receive payment will occur once after Emit invoice.


    2. Ship product must occur once after Confirm order.


  3. Archive order may occur once only after Emit invoice and Confirm order. Archive order must occur right before the end.
