Declare templates: Existence constraints

Init(a): Every process instance starts with activity a.
Init(a): Every process instance starts with activity a.
Participation(a): Activity a occurs at least once.
AtMostOne(a): Activity a occurs at most once.

Declare templates: Relation constraints I

Init(a): Every process instance starts with activity a.
Init(a): Every process instance starts with activity a.
Participation(a): Activity a occurs at least once.
AtMostOne(a): Activity a occurs at most once.

Declare templates: Relation constraints II

RespondedExistence(b, a): If b occurs, a has to occur at some point, no matter when
Precedence(a, b): If b occurs, then a has to occur before b
AlternatePrecedence(a, b): Each time b occurs, it is preceded by a, with no b in-between
ChainPrecedence(a, b): If b occurs, then a has to occur immediately beforehand

Declare templates: Mutual relation constraints

CoExistence(a, b): RespondedExistence (a, b) and RespondedExistence (b, a) hold
Succession(a, b): Response (a, b) and Precedence (b, a) hold
AlternateSuccession(a, b): AlternateResponse (a, b) and AlternatePrecedence (b, a) hold
ChainSuccession(a, b): ChainResponse (a, b) and ChainPrecedence (b, a) hold

Declare templates: Relation constraints II

NotCoExistence(a, b): a and b cannot co-occur
NotSuccession(a, b): b cannot occur after a and a cannot occur before b
NotChainSuccession(a, b): b cannot occur immediately after a

Constraints subsumption (strictness) hierarchy
